In the Lattice Boltzmann Method, each cell in the grid that represents our fluid is associated with nine floating point numbers that represent the state of the cell. In MRT we take these nine numbers that are in the frequency distribution space and convert them into the moment-space using a transformation matrix. Once in the moment-space we apply relaxation using a triangular matrix. We then convert the result back frequency distribution space by multiplying it by the inverse of the transformation matrix to get the final result.
